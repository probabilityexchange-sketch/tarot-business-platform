# SEO Strategy: "Psychological Tarot" Content Engine

## Targeted Keywords
- "tarot for shadow work"
- "psychological tarot reading"
- "narrative therapy with tarot"
- "tarot and creative writing"
- "using tarot for self-reflection"
- "psychology of tarot archetypes"

## The Content Engine (Administrator Side)
1. **Video Ingestion:** She records 10-15 minute videos on her phone/laptop.
2. **Transcription:** Use an AI tool (or manual transcription) to get the raw text.
3. **SEO Adaptation:** 
   - Use her M.F.A. skills (or yours) to polish the transcript into a long-form article.
   - Embed the YouTube video at the top of the blog post.
   - Use semantic HTML (`h1`, `h2`, `alt` tags) to maximize Google Search visibility.
4. **Interlinking:** Link every blog post to:
   - The 50-minute booking page.
   - The "Signature Course" waiting list/sales page.

## Automation & Tracking
- **Google Search Console:** Submit `sitemap.xml` (auto-generated by Next.js).
- **Google Analytics 4:** Track "Booking Clicks" and "Course Purchase" conversions.
- **Firebase:** Use Firestore to track which blog posts lead to the most newsletter signups.
