About Kali Meister

Intuitive Reader, Channel, & Spiritual Counselor

40+ Years of Practice

Kali Meister has spent over four decades studying and practicing the esoteric arts. Her path began in the hills of Tennessee, where she developed her gifts under traditional mentorship before going on to study depth psychology and comparative religion at the university level.

Today, she works with executives, creatives, entrepreneurs, and spiritual seekers from around the world — anyone who is ready to move beyond noise and get real clarity on what is happening beneath the surface of their life.

"Clarity isn't about seeing the future. It's about seeing your current landscape with enough depth that the right path becomes obvious."

Training & Disciplines

Kali's practice spans multiple divination traditions. She does not believe in siloing herself into one deck or one system — different tools reveal different layers of truth.

Tarot (Rider-Waite) Lenormand Astrology (Solar Fire) Channeling Oracle Cards Depth Psychology Spiritual Counseling

She is known for her direct, no-fluff approach. Clients often remark that she sees what they were hiding from themselves — and tells them plainly, with compassion.

A Note on Reading with Kali

All sessions are confidential, judgment-free, and focused on your highest good. Kali does not predict death or tragedy. She reads for clarity, empowerment, and next steps.

Sessions are available virtually via Zoom for clients worldwide, and in-person in the Chattanooga, TN area. A free 15-minute discovery call is offered to all new clients to ensure good fit before booking a full session.
