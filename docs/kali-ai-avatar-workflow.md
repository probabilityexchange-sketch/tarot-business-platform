# Kali AI Avatar Content Workflow
## kalimeister.com Short-Form Video Strategy

---

## Why an AI Avatar?

Kali's biggest constraint: she's "hard to pin down" for consistent content. Traditional social media requires:
- Showing up to film
- Multiple takes
- Editing time
- Consistent scheduling

AI avatar solves all of it. One script recorded, distributed everywhere, indefinitely.

**Tools:** HeyGen ($29/mo starter) or D-ID Studio ($29/mo) — both let you create a realistic talking avatar from text. Kali records one 5-minute video, they build a digital twin that speaks any script you feed it.

**Or:** No recording needed — use a stock avatar that looks the part (mysterious, warm, spiritual aesthetic) with Kali's real voice or a voice actor.

---

## The Avatar Setup (One-Time, ~2 Hours)

### Option A: HeyGen (Recommended)
1. Go to [heygen.com](https://heygen.com) → Sign up → Start free trial
2. Create a new "Photo Avatar" or "Video Avatar"
3. Upload a photo of Kali (professional headshot works best) OR
4. Record a 5-min video of Kali speaking naturally — HeyGen clones her voice too
5. Set voice to Kali's real voice or choose from their voice library
6. Download avatar video or connect to API for automated generation

### Option B: D-ID
1. Go to [d-id.com](https://d-id.com) → Create avatar from photo
2. Upload Kali's photo
3. Enter script → Generate talking video
4. No voice cloning — use AI voice from their library

### Option C: Free (Lowest Quality)
- Use ElevenLabs voice cloning + D-ID stock avatar
- Cost: ~$5/mo for ElevenLabs + free D-ID tier

**Recommended: HeyGen with Kali's real voice recording.** Quality matters for trust — this is a spiritual services business.

## Content Pillars (What the Avatar Talks About)

Each video follows ONE of these 5 content types:

### Pillar 1: Daily Card Draw (15-30 sec)
"Today's card for [zodiac sign / overall] is [CARD]. It means [brief 2-sentence interpretation]. What do you see in this card? Drop a comment and I'll pull a follow-up card for you."

**Why it works:** Algorithmically popular, builds parasocial connection, easy to batch produce.

### Pillar 2: "Did You Know?" Tarot Fact (15-20 sec)
"Did you know the Tower card isn't actually a bad card? It represents sudden change — and often, that's exactly what we need. Comment 'Tower' if this card has shown up for you recently."

**Why it works:** Debunking myths + engagement bait.

### Pillar 3: "Should I Do [X]?" Mini Reading (30-45 sec)
Reader holds up cards, gives a 3-card spread for someone asking "should I make this career change?" Quick, helpful, shareable.

**Why it works:** Directly answers what people are searching.

### Pillar 4: Client Transformation Story (30 sec)
"My client came to me feeling lost after a divorce. Three sessions later, she told me she finally felt like herself again. If you're in a similar place — let's talk."

**Why it works:** Social proof without violating confidentiality (generalized story).

### Pillar 5: Intuition Tip / Exercise (20-30 sec)
"Try this: before you make your next big decision, ask yourself — what does my gut say? Not your brain. Your gut. Write it down. Then ask again tomorrow."

**Why it works:** Actionable value, positions Kali as expert authority.

## Batch Production Workflow

**Every 2 weeks, produce 8-10 videos in one session.**

1. Pick 8 scripts from the script bank above
2. Open HeyGen or D-ID
3. Generate all videos (5-10 min total processing)
4. Download MP4s
5. Upload to Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
6. Schedule with Later or Buffer

**Tools needed:**
- HeyGen or D-ID account
- Later.com or Buffer (free tier) for scheduling
- Canva (free) for captions and thumbnails

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram Reels
- First 3 seconds are critical — hook with card visual or Kali's face
- Use trending audio if it fits the vibe
- Always include CTA: "Drop a comment," "Save this," "Link in bio"

### TikTok
- More forgiving of lower production quality
- Duets and stitch with spiritual accounts
- Sound-on mandatory — use captions overlay

### YouTube Shorts
- Longer retention = more推送 — aim for full 60 sec if content allows
- Better for "tarot reading" search intent
- Add card close-ups for visual interest

## Engagement Automation (Reply to Comments)

Kali shouldn't have to reply to every comment manually. Use:

**For Instagram:** Modoboats or ManyChat for auto-reply with keyword triggers
**For TikTok:** TikTok's built-in auto-reply + CommentGuard

Example auto-reply:
> "Thank you for your question! For a deeper look at your situation, I'd love to do a full reading with you. Book at kalimeister.com/readings 💫"
